Chitkara University

Chitkara University is a private university in India with its campus in Punjab. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields like Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, and other courses in Health Sciences, Nursing, Hospitality, Art & Design, Event Management, Mass Communication, etc.
